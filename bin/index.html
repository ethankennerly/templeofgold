Temple of Gold

Press ARROW KEYS to move.
Collect gold.
Remember the door.
Push the matching idol to open the door.
Each move costs one drop of lamp oil.
Collect all the gold and leave by the stairs.
To score bonus points, finish faster.

Weekend game jam by Ethan Kennerly, May 26, 2014.
Inspired by Speed Sort on Fit Brains.
