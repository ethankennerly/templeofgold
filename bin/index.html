Temple of Gold

Press ARROW KEYS to move.
Collect gold.
Remember the door.
Bring the key to open the door.
You can only carry one key at a time.
Each move costs one drop of lamp oil.
Collect all the gold and leave by the stairs.
To score bonus points, finish faster.

One day game jam by Ethan Kennerly, May 26, 2014.
Inspired by Dungeon of Death, Legend of Zelda, 
Maze Temple, A Maze Me, and Scuba Bear.
